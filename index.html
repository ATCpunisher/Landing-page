Our Services

1. Preparation of Technical Documentation

  • Environmental impact statements (individual and regional modality)
  • Technical justification study
  • Environmental risk studies
  • Preventive reports

2. Permit Management with SEMARNAT

  • Legal and technical advice to obtain environmental impact authorizations
  • Concession and procedures for ZOFEMAT and coastal environments
  • Waste management permits
  • Water discharge permits

3. Environmental Monitoring and Compliance

  • Follow-up on Terms and Conditions
  • Compliance audits
  • Environmental performance indicators
  • Regulatory compliance reporting

4. Environmental Studies and Land Use Planning

  • Ecological and urban carrying capacity assessment
  • Urban environmental feasibility of developments (residential, tourist, industrial)
  • Preventive reports to the Ministry of the Environment
  • Environmental management plans

5. Technical Support in Environmental Emergencies

  • Presentation of reports and corrective measures
  • Follow-up and resolution of procedures by PROFEPA
  • Spill response planning
  • Environmental contingency plans
