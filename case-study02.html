Sustainable Coastal Development in Quintana Roo

Environmental permitting and protection measures for a luxury resort with coral reef conservation

Client

Caribbean Tourism Group

Location

Riviera Maya, Quintana Roo

Duration

24 months

Services

EIA, Permits, Marine Protection

Project Summary

Forward Consultants Network led the environmental permitting process and designed mitigation measures for an exclusive luxury resort in Riviera Maya, with special focus on protecting adjacent coral reefs.

The project required a detailed Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), including oceanographic studies, monitoring of protected marine species, and current analysis to minimize impact on marine ecosystems. We implemented an innovative wastewater treatment system and a coral restoration program.

The development includes 120 luxury rooms, spa, restaurants and common areas designed with the highest standards of coastal sustainability, becoming a benchmark for responsible tourism in the region.

Key Project Data

  • Project Area 8 coastal hectares
  • Protected Reefs 2.5 km of coral barrier
  • Water Treatment Tertiary system with zero ocean discharge
  • Permits Obtained 15 from SEMARNAT, CONANP and PROFEPA
  • Transplanted Corals 1,200 colonies

Challenges & Solutions

Coral Reef Protection

The site bordered a protected reef area. We implemented sedimentation barriers during construction and a continuous coral monitoring program, plus transplanting affected colonies to safe zones.

Wastewater Management

We designed a tertiary treatment system with 100% water reuse for irrigation, eliminating any ocean discharge and protecting marine ecosystems.

Strict Coastal Regulations

Federal maritime-terrestrial zone regulations required design adjustments. We relocated non-essential structures and optimized space use to comply with all requirements.

Community Engagement

We organized workshops with local fishermen and tour operators to incorporate their concerns into the project design, while creating a local employment program.
