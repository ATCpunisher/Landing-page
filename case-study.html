Challenges & Solutions

Sensitive Ecosystem Protection

The site contained portions of a rare tropical dry forest ecosystem. Our team conducted extensive flora and fauna inventories, identifying three protected species that required special mitigation measures.

Community Concerns

Local residents were concerned about water usage and traffic impacts. We organized community workshops and adjusted project plans to include a rainwater capture system and improved access roads.

Archaeological Findings

Initial surveys revealed pre-Columbian artifacts. We worked with INAH to document and preserve findings while adjusting site plans to avoid sensitive areas.

Permitting Complexity

The project required coordination between federal, state, and municipal agencies. Our established relationships and expertise streamlined the approval process.
